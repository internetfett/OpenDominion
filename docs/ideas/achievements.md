# Achievement Ideas

Ideas for achievements that don't overlap with Daily Rankings or Valhalla (which cover quantitative leaderboard metrics like most land, networth, attacks, spying, magic, resources, etc.). These focus on qualitative milestones, behaviors, and memorable moments.

## Milestones (participation/longevity)
- **Veteran** - Completed 5 rounds
- **Old Guard** - Completed 10 rounds
- **Founding Member** - Played in Round 1 (or first N rounds)
- **Iron Will** - Finished a round without being successfully invaded
- **Endurance** - Logged in every day of a round
- **Full House** - Played every race at least once

## Social/Realm
- **Team Player** - Cast 50+ friendly spells on realmmates in a round
- **Diplomat** - Served as monarch for an entire round
- **Elected Official** - Won a monarchy vote
- **United Front** - Member of a realm that destroyed a wonder together
- **War Council** - Part of a realm that won a war

## Comebacks & Clutch Moments
- **Underdog** - Successfully invaded a dominion 50%+ larger than you
- **Comeback Kid** - Finished in the top 5 after being below rank 50 at mid-round
- **Last Stand** - Successfully defended against 3+ invasions in a single day
- **Razor's Edge** - Won an invasion with less than 1% margin

## Playstyle / Strategy
- **Pacifist** - Reached 3000+ acres without invading anyone
- **Explorer** - Explored 1000+ acres in a single round
- **Warmonger** - Launched 20+ successful invasions in a round
- **Architect** - Built 5000+ buildings in a round
- **Scholar** - Unlocked all techs in a round
- **Ascendant** - Reached max hero level in a round

## Espionage & Magic
- **Untouchable** - Finished a round with 0 successful spy/wizard ops against you
- **Spellweaver** - Had 10+ active spells simultaneously
- **Double Agent** - Charmed 50+ spies in a round
- **Counterintelligence** - Executed 20+ spies in a round

## Economic
- **Midas Touch** - Held 1M+ platinum at once
- **Hoarder** - Had max capacity in all resources simultaneously
- **Investor** - Spent 500K+ on castle improvements in a round

## Rare/Hidden
- **Phoenix** - Rebuilt to 75%+ of your peak land after losing 33%+
- **Speedrunner** - Reached 2000 acres before day 20
- **Perfectionist** - Finished a round with 0 failed invasions, 0 failed spy ops, 0 failed spells
